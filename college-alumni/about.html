Our Mission

Our alumni network strives to build a strong and supportive community that connects, inspires, and empowers our graduates. We aim to foster lifelong relationships and provide valuable resources for professional and personal growth.

Our History

Founded in 1960, our college has nurtured countless leaders and innovators. The alumni network was established in 1985 with a vision to connect graduates and continue their engagement with the college community.

Objectives

  • Provide networking opportunities for professional growth.
  • Facilitate mentorship programs to guide young alumni.
  • Organize events that celebrate and unite our alumni.
  • Support alumni-driven initiatives and projects.
